About

Sonia has a diverse background in the beauty world. Graduated with an Esthetics certificate from Eastern Suffolk Boces and a Makeup Artistry certification from MAC Makeup, she has an array of knowledge involving makeup techniques, skills, and safety practices. In addition, Sonia has worked as a Cosmetic Chemist which required creating makeup from raw chemicals. She used her talented eye for color to create color matching projects from scratch and continues to sharpen her recognition of different undertones in makeup. She has also worked at three haunted houses as a Makeup Artist which required using SFX Makeup techniques, prosthetics, fake blood, latex, and face painting skills to create various complete horror looks for many scare actors.

From a Biology and Esthetics perspective, Sonia knows that facial bone anatomy, bone structure, face shape, eye/lip/nose/brow shape, and the natural contours of a persons face need to be taken into consideration when doing makeup to enhance a person's natural beauty.
